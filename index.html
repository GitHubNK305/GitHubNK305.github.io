Welcome to my Website.

I am Jin Tao, currently serving as a Senior AI Scientist at Silo AI Oy. With over 15 years of dedicated experience in the Artificial Intelligence field, I bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the forefront. My academic journey includes a role as an Associate Professor at the College of Artificial Intelligence at Nankai University, a postdoctoral researcher at Aalto University, Finland, and a 'Boya' research fellow at Peking University, one of the top-ranking institutions in the world. In recognition of my research contributions, I was appointed as the Academy of Finland Postdoctoral Research Fellow in 2018.

My research endeavors are focused on complex system dynamics, intelligent control, smart systems, reinforcement learning, and machine learning. To date, I've published over 110 papers in esteemed peer-reviewed journals and conferences, accumulating more than 900 citations and boasting an H-index of 16. My dedication to staying current in the field is evident through my Microsoft certifications, where I am honored to hold titles as a Microsoft Certified Azure AI Engineer Associate and a Microsoft Certified Azure Data Scientist Associate. It is my pleasure to continue pushing the boundaries of Artificial Intelligence, a field I am passionate about and deeply committed to.
